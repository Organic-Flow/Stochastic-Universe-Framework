Technical Due Diligence
Q

In the JSON data, the `stochastic_wave` mode did not hit the 0.001 threshold (it stayed at 0.014). Isn't this an algorithmic failure?

A

Not at all. It is a precise confirmation of the mathematical design. The topological mode is strictly deterministic (everyone is blindly attracted to their closest neighbours), so it converges ultra-fast to absolute zero, often ending in total structural "Mode Collapse". The stochastic_wave mode, however, deliberately introduces a "Wave Noise" factor and quantum tunneling probabilities. This noise aggressively prevents the internal variance from falling below a certain livable threshold (~0.014). It keeps the network conceptually "alive" and highly receptive to new incoming stimuli instead of freezing entirely into a dead state. And even then, its AUC convergence is still 2.3x faster than the purely Random Baseline.

Q

So convergence is achieved entirely without Backpropagation, purely through "Attraction"?

A

Precisely. It is Unsupervised Local Hebbian Attraction. We do absolutely not need to explicitly compute and propagate a Loss Error backward through sequential layers via heavy chain-rule derivatives. Each individual cell locally corrects its coordinate position strictly by "looking" only at its k closest topological neighbours. Because this micro-correction occurs simultaneously and horizontally across all nodes, the macro-system achieves total equilibrium exponentially faster than serial computation.
